Chris Hani

Here is a time in the life of Chris Hani

  • 1942 - Thembisile Hani was born on 28 June
  • Age of 15 - Joined the ANC Youth League
  • 1960s - He received military training in the Soviet Union
  • 1982 - Moved to the ANC's headquarters in Lusaka, Zambia.
  • 1990 - Permanently returned to South Africa following the unbanning of the ANC
  • 1993 - Chris was assassinated outside his house
